AI learns from what's publicly available on the internet. If your product has a low score, it usually means one of these:

  • Not enough public content — reviews, blog posts, forum mentions about your product are sparse
  • Vague product description — AI doesn't have a clear category or use-case to attach to your product
  • New product — AI training data hasn't caught up yet
  • No llms.txt or schema markup — AI crawlers can't easily parse what your product does
